## Executive Summary

Pinpoint is pivoting from a Chrome Extension feedback tool into a full SaaS platform competing with Marker.io, BugHerd, and Userback. The visual feedback market is ~$1.2B (2026). We differentiate on real-time collaboration (Socket.IO), zero-friction capture (Chrome Extension, no code install), and a generous free tier for PLG.

**Strategic Goal:** 2-3% of SAM (~$16-24M) within 24 months.

## Business Phases

### Phase 1: SaaS Foundation (Months 1-6)
Billing (Stripe), multi-tenancy, teams, permissions, onboarding, dashboard V2.
**Exit:** 50 paying customers.

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-14)
Integrations (Jira, Slack, Linear, GitHub), workflows, client portal, video recording, notifications.
**Exit:** 500 paying customers, NPS >40.

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 15-24)
Public API, white-label, enterprise (SSO, audit logs, SCIM), SDK, compliance (SOC 2).
**Exit:** 5 enterprise contracts >$1K/mo.

### Phase 4: Platform (Months 25-36)
Marketplace, AI (auto-categorization, duplicate detection), analytics, partner program.
**Exit:** $3M+ ARR, 20+ marketplace plugins.

## Go-to-Market

- **PLG:** Free tier → viral loop (annotate → share → teammate signs up)
- **Content:** "Feedback workflow" blog series, comparison pages
- **Communities:** Dev.to, Reddit r/webdev, Hacker News
- **Product Hunt:** Launch at Month 4
- **Paid:** Google Ads on competitor keywords

## KPIs

- **North Star:** Weekly Active Annotations per Team
- Free→Paid conversion: 5-8%
- Monthly churn: <6% (Y1), <4% (Y2)
- Time to first annotation: <5 min
- NPS: 40+ (Y1), 55+ (Y2)
